Is money always the most important thing when choosing a job. Discuss.

Salary is regarded as one of the most important factors while making a choice between vacancies. What is more, for many people it becomes a major criterion for the prospects of the workplace and comes in first place in the search for work. However, a growing number of people feel that job satisfaction and career growth are also necessary aspects of the job to be considered.

First of all, it is a proven fact that job satisfaction will make people working with efficiency and productivity. It includes a great diversity of parameters such as work-life balance, vacation, comfortable office, and so on. For instance, modern people tend to spend enough time with their family, so it is essential for them that the office is not far from home and commute is great. Furthermore, a person working in a friendly atmosphere, without night shifts and weekend work, will be happier than the person who going through extreme stress and pressure in a company. Thus, job satisfaction is a significant factor while choosing a job.

Secondly, when it comes to select a workplace career growth has a great importance for many people. Promotions, new skills, job responsibilities are some of the parts of it. People tend to give meaning to these attributes of a vacancy as it becomes vital for their future career. To give an illustration, suppose that some companies cannot offer high salaries, but they have interesting projects. In this way, working on them, a person can develop useful skills and gain the necessary experience. For this reason, career prospects overshadow high salaries for some people.

In conclusion, for many people money is the most important thing when choosing a job. However, there are plenty people for whom job satisfaction and career growth are priority because they help maintain a work-life balance, be more productive, and also gain the necessary skills and experience.
